Refund Policy

Refund requests are reviewed on a case-by-case basis.

1. Eligible Refunds

A refund may be considered if the service is materially unavailable, not delivered as described, or subject to a verified billing error.

2. Time Window

Customers should submit refund requests within 7 days of the original charge unless otherwise required by applicable law.

3. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Normal usage consumption under an active plan
  • Change of mind after purchase
  • Violations of service terms or abuse of the platform
  • Failure to use the service after access has been provided

4. Billing Providers

Refund handling may be processed through the authorized billing provider used for your purchase. Provider rules and payment processor requirements may also apply.

5. Contact

If you need support regarding a billing issue, use the support or contact method provided during onboarding or account setup.
